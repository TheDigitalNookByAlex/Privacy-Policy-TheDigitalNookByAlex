Privacy Policy

TheDigitalNookByAlex — Last updated: June 4, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how TheDigitalNookByAlex handles information when you use our Pinterest auto-pinning application in connection with our Etsy shop.

Information We Access

  • Pinterest account information (board names, pin data) solely for creating pins from our Etsy listings.
  • Etsy listing data (titles, descriptions, images) to generate Pinterest pins that promote our products.

How We Use This Information

  • To create and schedule Pinterest pins from our Etsy shop listings.
  • To manage Pinterest boards and organize pins by product category.
  • To analyze which products generate the most engagement.

Data Storage & Security

All API tokens are stored securely and used exclusively for the automated pinning workflow. We do not sell, share, or distribute any personal data to third parties.

Data Retention

We retain Pinterest access tokens only as long as the App is active. You can revoke access at any time through your Pinterest app settings.
